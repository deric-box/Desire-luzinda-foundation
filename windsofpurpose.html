WINDS OF PURPOSE

Empowering Youth to Discover Purpose, Potential, and a Future of Impact

Empowering Youth to Discover Purpose, Potential, and a Future of Impact.

The Winds of Purpose Program is a youth empowerment initiative under the Desire Luzinda Foundation, created to guide young people toward discovering their purpose, building their confidence, and unlocking their full potential.

We believe every young person carries a unique gift and calling. Through mentorship, education, and practical skills training, Winds of Purpose helps them rise above limitations, discover direction, and pursue a meaningful life.

This program is designed to shape youths into purpose-driven, skilled, and responsible individuals ready to positively influence their communities.

Our Focus Areas

  • Purpose Discovery & Mentorship: We provide mentorship sessions, coaching programs, and purpose-discovery workshops that help youths understand who they are, their strengths, and their God-given potential.
  • Skills Training & Empowerment: Winds of Purpose equips youths with hands-on vocational skills such as:
    • Tailoring & Fashion
    • Baking & Culinary Arts
    • Hairdressing & Beauty
    • Digital Literacy
    • Entrepreneurship
    These skills empower young people to become self-reliant and employable.
  • Education Support: We offer academic assistance, tuition support (where needed), scholastic materials, and guidance for youths pursuing formal education as part of their journey toward a purposeful future.
  • Leadership Development: Through leadership clinics, seminars, and youth forums, we build confident leaders who can influence their communities with wisdom, integrity, and impact.
  • Community Engagement & Collaboration: The program collaborates with schools, youth groups, churches, and community organizations to reach more young people and broaden opportunities for training and mentorship.

Our Impact

  • Youths discovering their identity, direction, and purpose
  • Increased livelihood opportunities through vocational skills
  • Improved confidence, resilience, and leadership abilities
  • Transformed young people contributing positively in their communities
  • A growing movement of empowered and purpose-driven youths

How you can Support

You can be part of shaping the next generation by:

  • Sponsoring a youth through skilling
  • Donating training materials or equipment
  • Supporting youth scholarships
  • Partnering with the program monthly
  • Offering mentorship, internships, or training opportunities

Our Vision: raise a generation of purposeful, skilled, and empowered youth who know their worth, live intentionally, and lead change in their communities and beyond.
