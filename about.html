Who we Are

A CALL TO SERVE, A CALL TO SAVE.

Desire Luzinda Foundation is a non-profit empowerment and support organization that has over time set its goal on changingand impacting lives.

With a divine calling, Desire Luzinda foundation has shared commitment to relieve suffering and improve the lives of Children based on non-profit activities and initiatives it conducts.
We are a foundation that has set up a mission to provide health care and education assistance to most in need.
Founded in 2015, under divine call to serve, give hope to orphaned and underprivileged children in various ways and areas.

We attribute all these achievements to the revelation of the Love of Christ as to love is to live and to live is to love John 3:16 NKJV “For God so loved the world that He gave His only begotten Son, that whoever believes in Him should not perish but have everlasting life.”
By God's Grace, we aim to extend God's love and mercy to as many in need in a hope which is not a human hope; a Hope beyond the physical needs, a Hope that is anchored in Christ Jesus.

Cosmetologist

I was found in a state of depression and fighting addictions, had dropped out of university.By God's Grace through Mama Dee (Mrs. Lubyogo), I was able to get over all the addictions and taken back to school at Zziwa Hair Academy where I attained a diploma in Cosmetology, am very grateful to DLF and the Woman of God Mrs. Desire.

Emmanuel Wamala

Civil Engineer

Growing up in a family of 11 children, my mother's salary could not cater for all our basic needs. I had to sell food in the market to assist my mother and fend for my siblings. Through DLF, I was able to get sponsorship to join Makererere University, pursued a Diploma in Civil Engineering and today I am a proud civil engineer. Thank you DLF for the support.
