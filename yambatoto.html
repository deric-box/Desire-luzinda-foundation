YAMBA TOTO

Transforming Vulnerable Children into Hopeful Futures

Every contribution makes a lifelong difference.

The Yamba Toto Program is a child-support initiative under the Desire Luzinda Foundation, dedicated to restoring dignity, safety, and opportunity to orphaned and less-advantaged children. Through Yamba Toto, we provide a loving home, basic needs, education support, and emotional care—empowering every child to thrive.

Our Focus Areas

  • Shelter & Safe Homes: Through our Yamba Toto Homes, children receive a secure, nurturing place to live — a place where they are protected, cared for, and given a sense of belonging.
  • Basic Needs & Welfare Support: We ensure access to food, clothing, hygiene essentials, medical care, and everyday necessities that enable healthy, stable growth.
  • Education Support: Yamba Toto provides school fees, scholastic materials, uniforms, mentorship, and learning resources to open doors of opportunity for every child.
  • Emotional & Spiritual Care: Our caregivers offer mentorship, counseling, and spiritual guidance to nurture confidence, resilience, and emotional wellbeing.
  • Partnerships & Community Outreach: We collaborate with orphanages, child-care institutions, and community leaders to support more vulnerable children with supplies, programs, and empowerment tools.

Our Impact

  • Children gaining access to education and completing school
  • Vulnerable children restored to stable, loving environments
  • Strengthened community orphanages through partnerships
  • Improved physical, emotional, and spiritual wellbeing
  • A growing network of supported children with renewed hope and purpose

How you can Support

Your partnership helps transform lives. You can support Yamba Toto by:

  • Sponsoring a child
  • Donating food, clothes, or scholastic materials
  • Supporting our Yamba Toto Homes
  • Becoming a monthly program partner
  • Volunteering or collaborating as an organization

Our Vision: is to see every vulnerable child in Uganda raised in love, dignity, and opportunity — free from neglect and empowered to achieve their full potential.
